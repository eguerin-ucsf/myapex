Advanced Search Tips

blood OR "medical assistant"
Searches for documents that contain either the word "blood" or the exact phrase "medical assistant"

blood OR medical OR assistant
Searches for documents that contain any of the words: "blood", "medical", or "assistant"

blood AND "medical assistant"
Searches for documents that contain both the word "blood" and the exact phrase "medical assistant"

blood NOT "medical assistant"
Searches for documents that contain the word "blood" and do not contain the exact phrase "medical assistant"

(blood OR nurse) AND patient
Searches for documents that contain either the word "blood" or the word "nurse" and the word "patient"
